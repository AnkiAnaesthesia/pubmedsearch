Automated Quality Checklists
— run automatically on any paper with full text available
Once a paper's full text is retrieved (via PubMed Central or a free PDF), the app will automatically run quality checklists against the text. Each checklist item is keyword-scanned and pre-filled as a starting point — you can review and adjust each item manually inside the paper's expanded view. Checklists are matched to study type by default but can be changed per paper. RoB2 is always assessed alongside the matched checklist.
✓ CONSORT 2010 — RCTs ✓ RoB2 — All trials (risk of bias) ✓ PRISMA 2020 — Systematic Reviews & Meta-Analyses ✓ STROBE — Observational Studies ✓ CARE — Case Reports ✓ AGREE II — Clinical Guidelines
